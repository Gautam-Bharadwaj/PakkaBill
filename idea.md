# PakkaBill | Executive Invoicing & AI Financial Intelligence

## Project Idea
PakkaBill is a high-performance, industrial-grade full-stack billing and inventory management ecosystem designed specifically for elite wholesalers, manufacturers, and large-scale distributors. It replaces legacy, fragmented systems with a unified "Executive Console" that combines transaction management with predictive AI intelligence.

## Scope
The project encompasses a robust Node.js/Express backend following enterprise design patterns and a high-fidelity React Native (Expo) mobile application. The target scope is to handle the complete financial lifecycle of a wholesale business—from onboarding dealers and managing complex SKUs to generating GST-compliant sequential invoices and tracking multi-stage payments.

## Key Features
1. **Executive Dashboard**: A real-time command center showing revenue growth (MTD), pending receivables heatmaps, and sales velocity metrics.
2. **AI Intelligence Layer (RAG)**: A dedicated AI assistant that uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation to answer complex financial queries like "What is my total due from high-risk accounts?" or "Predict next month's product demand."
3. **Sequential Invoice Engine**: Automated generation of corporate-grade PDFs with unique, sequential numbering and Smart GST calculation.
4. **Dual-Action WhatsApp Integration**: One-tap professional sharing that sends the PDF document and simultaneously copies professional transaction summaries to the clipboard.
5. **Financial Lifecycle Tracking**: Granular management of partial payments, balance re-calculations, and invoice status transitions (Pending, Partially Paid, Paid).
6. **Stock Guard Inventory**: Real-time SKU tracking with low-stock alerts and automated inventory adjustment upon invoice creation.
